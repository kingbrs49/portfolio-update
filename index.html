Paulo C Camacho


Bio


With a plethora of knowledge over a wide array of academic interests, Paulo has found himself in the exciting and lucrative world of computer programming.

Born in the Philippines, Paulo moved to the States in 1984, and lived in Davis, California for most of his life. Despite his nearly 35-year stay in the US, he continues to strive for his permanent residency, and hopes to attain this through his newfound foray into programming.

Paulo is a passionate sports fan, and a loyal follower of the Sacramento Kings, San Francisco 49ers, and San Francisco Giants. He will talk your ear off about sports if you let him, and he admits that the best sports memory of his life (so far) is when he watched the Giants win the National League Pennant at AT&T Park on the anniversary of his brother's wedding in 2014.

He also enjoys YouTube in his spare time -- in fact, his love for YouTube and sports has taken him to VidCon for four years straight (side note: his favorite YouTuber is Philip DeFranco), and has two sports YouTube channels: The Macho Sports Report, and SecondHand Sports.
